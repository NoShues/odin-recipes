Cannoli Dip

Description

Jen discovered this recipe from a co-worker and it has become a family favorite.

Laced with chocolate chips and served with broken waffle cones, this cannoli dip is guaranteed to be a party favorite!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ricotta
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup mini chocolate chips
  • waffle cones, broken into dippable pieces

Steps

  1. Using a stand mixer (or a hand mixer + large bowl), beat ricotta, cream cheese, and powdered sugar on medium-high speed until smooth, about 3-5 minutes. Add in vanilla extract and whip for another 30 seconds.
  2. Taste test the dip, if you would like it sweeter, add more powdered sugar, about 2 tablespoons at a time. You can safely add up to another Â½ cup of powdered sugar (8 tablespoons) without negatively impacting consistency.
  3. Fold in mini chocolate chips, reserving some to sprinkle on top for decoration, if desired.
  4. Serve dip immediately with broken waffle cones for dipping.
